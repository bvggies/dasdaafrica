Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DaSDA Africa

What is DaSDA Africa?

+

DaSDA Africa (Disability and Social Development Advocacy Africa), also known as Positive Impact, is a renowned NGO with 18 years of experience in advocating for the rights and freedoms of individuals with disabilities and the less privileged. Founded in 2007, we work to create a society where equality thrives through advocacy, inclusion, and social development.

What is your mission?

+

Our mission is to empower individuals with disabilities through advocacy, inclusion, and social development, creating a more accessible and equitable Africa for all. We work towards building an inclusive society where individuals with disabilities can thrive, supported by advocacy, accessibility, and social development.

What programs do you offer?

+

We offer several key programs including:

  • Charity & Essential Items: Providing charity and essential items to those in need
  • Entrepreneurship Support: Offering startup capital to empower entrepreneurs with disabilities
  • Infrastructure Development: Constructing infrastructure projects to improve community facilities
  • Advocacy & Policy Change: Working to influence policies that benefit individuals with disabilities

How can I get involved?

+

There are many ways to get involved with DaSDA Africa:

  • Volunteer: Join our team as a volunteer and contribute your skills and time
  • Donate: Support our work financially through one-time or recurring donations
  • Partner: If you represent an organization, consider partnering with us
  • Advocate: Help spread awareness about disability rights and inclusion
  • Contact Us: Reach out through our contact page to discuss how you can contribute

How can I donate?

+

You can donate to DaSDA Africa through our Donate page. We accept various forms of donations including:

  • Online donations through secure payment gateways
  • Bank transfers
  • Mobile money payments
  • In-kind donations (essential items, equipment, etc.)

All donations go directly towards supporting our programs and initiatives. Visit our Donate page for more information.

Where do you operate?

+

DaSDA Africa is based in Ghana, West Africa. We operate primarily in Ghana, working with communities across the country. Our advocacy work also extends to regional and continental levels, particularly through our involvement in the African Disability Protocol campaign.

Who are your partners?

+

We work with several strategic partners including:

  • Yaa Nhyira Foundation
  • Alem Foundation
  • Dels Foundation
  • Universal Peace Federation
  • ADD International

These partnerships enable us to amplify our impact and reach more communities.

How can I stay updated with your work?

+

You can stay updated with our work through:

  • Our Blogs page where we share news and updates
  • Following us on social media (links available in footer)
  • Subscribing to our newsletter (coming soon)
  • Visiting our Gallery to see photos from our events and programs

Do you provide direct services to individuals?

+

Yes, we provide direct services to individuals with disabilities through our various programs. This includes providing essential items, offering startup capital for entrepreneurs, and supporting community infrastructure projects. We also engage in advocacy work to create systemic change that benefits all individuals with disabilities.
