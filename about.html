About Us

18 Years of Dedicated Service to Disability Advocacy

Our History

Disability and Social Development Advocacy Africa, known as Positive Impact, is a renowned NGO with 18 years of experience in advocating for the rights and freedoms of the less privileged. Founded in 2007, our organization has been driven by a clear vision of creating a society where equality thrives.

Our origins are rooted in a passion for social equity. Over the years, we have evolved from a grassroots initiative into a dynamic advocacy organization, while remaining firmly anchored in our founding vision: to contribute meaningfully to the common good by promoting equality and empowerment for all.

Since our inception, we have employed creative approaches to address social issues, including music and storytelling, to inspire change and motivate action. Through strategic partnerships with organizations such as Yaa Nhyira Foundation, Alem Foundation, Dels Foundation, Universal Peace Federation, and ADD International, we have implemented various programs and projects to provide essential support to vulnerable populations and also advocate for policy change.
